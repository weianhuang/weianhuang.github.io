Exploding Blackjack

  • Code
  • Demo

What

A text-based terminal game integrating Exploding Kittens and Blackjack with save/load, multi-player, and pseudo-graphics capabilities.

Why

Under the limitation of a text-based terminal game that is suitable, we wanted to create a simple card game with an innovative twist. Since the main gameplay mechanic for both Blackjack and Exploding Kittens is drawing cards, we decided to blend the two together into a new game.

How

This game is invented, designed, and implemented by a team of 3. The game is made with Ocaml with the AI following preset rules.
